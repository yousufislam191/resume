Research Activity

Major Challenges & Proposed Solution for 6G from Bangladesh Ecosystem Perspective

Ongoing Research

THIS TOPIC WAS CHOSEN FOR MY THESIS COURSE

6G is the next generation cellular communication technology. We are moving towards high bandwidth wireless communication day after day. At the same time, industries are keen to use low latency and high bandwidth. A lot of research has been done on this and research is still ongoing. We know from various studies that 6G will be implemented by the end of 2030. I and two other members of my group (e.g. Naima Akhtar and Imam Hossain) are analyzing the results of previous work to find out what challenges the 6G may face in the context of Bangladesh's ecosystem and after verifying various results with the obtained dataset, we are trying to find a better solution.
